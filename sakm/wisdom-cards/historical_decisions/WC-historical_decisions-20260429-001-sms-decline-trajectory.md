# Legacy SMS Carrier Decline — Existential Context for AI Pivot

## Context
ANTS's historical cash engine (SMS messaging via True/Dtac/Tech Autosol carriers) is contracting at carrier-level rates that no sales effort can reverse. April 2026 marked the fourth consecutive month of decline. The 4-product AI portfolio (Training · AI Chat SME · AI Assistant · CRM+CDP+AI) is the planned successor revenue stream — and the clock for that succession is running.

## Decline Numbers (YTD APR vs prior comparable period)

| Carrier | YTD Volume (THB) | YoY Growth |
|---------|------------------|-----------|
| True | 178,508,355 | **-19.20%** |
| Dtac | 36,171,747 | **-68.92%** |
| Tech Autosol | 30,899,162 | **-9.97%** |
| **Total** | **245,579,264** | **-17.75%** |

### Segment Detail
- **Credit Topup:** 194M YTD · +7.73% YoY · BUT -63.27% MoM in April
- **SMS share of Communication Team:** 95.17% (44.10M of 46.34M)
- Other channels: Line OA 2.05M · CDP 1.20M · MMS 0.60M · LON 0.26M · Media 0.10M

## Strategic Implications

### Why this is existential, not just a sales problem
1. **Dtac -68.92%** — this scale of decline is not a sales execution issue. It's carrier-level structural shift (regulatory · spam restrictions · OTT messaging displacement).
2. **95% concentration in SMS** — Communication Team has no diversification buffer. If SMS continues at -17.75% pace, the team breaks pace within 2 quarters.
3. **April MoM Credit Topup -63.27%** — even within SMS, the cash-cow segment cracked sharply this month.

### Why the AI pivot is the canonical answer
- AI Chat SME directly substitutes the SMS use case (customer messaging) with higher margin and modern channel (LINE/Facebook)
- CRM+CDP+AI captures the data/automation revenue SMS can't reach
- Government team's pipeline (Smart City · ภัยพิบัติ · จราจร · กยศ Chatbot) is AI-native, not SMS-extensible

## Lessons Learned
1. **Concentrated cash cows hide vulnerability.** 95% reliance on one product means no shock absorber when carrier-level shifts occur.
2. **YoY ≠ MoM signal.** Credit Topup is +7.73% YoY but -63.27% MoM. Trust the recent number — it's the leading indicator.
3. **Carrier-level declines cannot be sold against.** Sales effort is wasted; product pivot is the only response.
4. **The successor product needs revenue before the predecessor breaks.** Current state: AI products at 9.6% of target while SMS holds 95% of CO Team. Gap will narrow either by AI ramping up or SMS collapsing further.
5. **Government pipeline is AI-native, not legacy.** The 30M+ Government pipeline is structurally different from carrier SMS — no carrier-level commodity risk.
