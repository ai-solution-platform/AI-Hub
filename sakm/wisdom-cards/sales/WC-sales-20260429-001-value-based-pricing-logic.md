# Value-Based Pricing Logic

## Context
The hook is not "AI features" but "headcount math." Customer pays an admin THB 15-25K/month. ANTS sells AI at THB 5-15K/month. The customer pockets THB 8-15K monthly while gaining 24-hour coverage. ROI is mechanical, not aspirational.

## Pricing Structure (3 Products with Different Logic)

### AI Chat SME — Subscription
- **Starter:** 2,990 THB/mo · chat + FAQ
- **Growth:** 5,990 THB/mo · sales + recommend
- **Pro:** 9,900 THB/mo · automation + CRM sync
- **Add-ons:** LINE/FB +1K · Training +2K · Broadcast +3K
- **Trial:** 7-14 days free · close via landing page

### AI Assistant (Vela) — Setup Fee + Subscription
- **Pilot:** 100-300K (1 use case)
- **Business:** 300-800K (2-3 workflows)
- **Enterprise:** 1M+ (full org)
- **Subscription:** 20-100K/mo per user/usage
- **Logic:** "Cut 1 employee 20K/mo · AI uses only 8K"

### CRM+CDP+AI — Project + License
- **Project:** 500K-3M+ implementation
- **License:** 50-300K/mo ongoing
- **Add-ons:** Data Integration +200-500K · AI Model +100-300K · Dashboard +100K+
- **Bundle hint:** Sell with AI Assistant to lower friction

### Training/Consulting — Per Session / Retainer
- **Workshop 1d:** 50-150K (market opener)
- **Corporate Training:** 150-500K (in-house)
- **Consulting:** 50-200K/mo (roadmap retainer)
- **Project:** 500K+/proj
- **Trick:** "เรียนฟรี ถ้าซื้อระบบ" — discount training fee against big deal

## Lessons Learned
1. **Anchor to headcount math.** Customer brain knows admin cost. Compare AI cost to admin cost, not feature count.
2. **Trial mechanics matter.** 7-14 day free trial closed via landing page = self-serve funnel for SME tier. No sales call needed.
3. **Bundle pricing for enterprise.** AI Assistant + CRM+CDP bundled lowers per-product friction · increases TCV.
4. **Free training as a deal-closer.** Customer wants training. Make it the gift that anchors the big project.
5. **Don't quote AI features.** Customer doesn't buy NLP/LLM. Customer buys "save 1 person" or "reply 24 hours" or "increase sales 20%."
