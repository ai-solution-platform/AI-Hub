# 4-Step Sales Close Script

## Context
Customer doesn't buy AI. Customer buys "profit + time + reduced headache." The 4-step script forces the conversation to stay on the customer's pain — not on ANTS features.

## The Script

### Step 1 — Pain
> "ตอนนี้แอดมินตอบลูกค้าทันไหม?"

Forces the customer to describe the existing problem in their own words. Don't move on until they've answered.

### Step 2 — Impact
> "เสียลูกค้าไปกี่ % เพราะตอบช้า?"

Pushes them to quantify the loss. Even if they guess, the number becomes the anchor for the value calculation.

### Step 3 — Solution
> "AI ตอบแทน 24 ชม. + ปิดการขาย"

Position the outcome — 24-hour coverage AND deal closing. Two outcomes in one line. Don't mention LLM/NLP/chatbot tech.

### Step 4 — Close
> "ลอง Pilot 14 วัน — ไม่เวิร์คไม่คิดเงิน"

De-risk completely. The customer's only loss is 14 days. Zero capital downside.

## Funnel Math (need 500 leads to close 10-20)
| Stage | Conversion | Volume |
|-------|-----------|--------|
| Awareness (content/ads) | — | 500 leads |
| Interest → Demo (webinar) | 20% | 100 |
| Workshop (training as lead gen) | 30% | 40 |
| Pilot (AI Chat / AI Assistant) | 40% | 20 |
| Close + Upsell | 50% | ~10 |

## Lessons Learned
1. **Customer must speak first.** Step 1 is a question — not a pitch. If the customer doesn't describe pain in their own words, you have no anchor.
2. **Quantify before solving.** Step 2 forces a number. Without a number, the value calc is hand-wavy.
3. **Outcomes, not features.** Step 3 is two outcomes — coverage + revenue. Tech jargon kills the close.
4. **Zero-risk pilot is the unlock.** Step 4 removes the customer's downside entirely. 14 days vs. 12-month commitment.
5. **Funnel needs 50x lead-to-close.** 500 leads → 10 closes. CPL math must support this ratio.
