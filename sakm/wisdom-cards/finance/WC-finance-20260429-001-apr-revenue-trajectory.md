# ANTS APR 2026 Revenue Trajectory

## Context
At end of April 2026 (4/12 of fiscal year elapsed = 33% pro-rata), ANTS achieved 21.8% of its annual OKR target. The 11.2-point gap is driven primarily by Government team underperformance (1.67%) and Martech team ramp delay (9.6%), partially offset by Communication team holding 58% pace.

## Key Numbers (CONFIDENTIAL — not for external sharing)
- **OKR YTD:** THB 21.8M / 100M (21.8% · expected 33% pro-rata)
- **Gross revenue YTD:** THB 51M (Jan 10.52M · Feb 11.80M · Mar 13.63M · Apr 13.81M)
- **Communication team:** 17.53M / 30M (58.43%) — only team on-pace
- **Government team:** 0.50M / 30M (1.67%) — needs 2.46M/month for next 8 months
- **Martech team:** 3.85M / 40M (9.6%) · 39.2% profit margin · 19 deals · avg ~200K/deal
- **SMS provider growth:** True -19.20% · Dtac -68.92% · Tech Autosol -9.97% · Total -17.75%

## Lessons Learned
1. **Pro-rata math is the honest metric** — gross 51M sounds healthy until you discount for non-OKR-qualifying revenue. Dashboard must lead with OKR-qualifying number, not gross.
2. **Single-team dependency is fragile** — Communication 58% on-pace vs others <10% means one team is carrying. Diversification urgent.
3. **Carrier-level decline is structural** — Dtac -68.92% isn't a sales failure, it's a market shift. Cannot be reversed by sales effort.
4. **Pipeline ≠ revenue** — Government team has 30M+ visible pipeline but 1.67% closed. Procurement cycle is the bottleneck, not demand.
5. **High-margin teams can be small-revenue** — Martech 39.2% margin but 9.6% target progress. Margin alone is not enough; volume must scale.

## Application — Target Agents
- **CFO #7:** Use as runway baseline · model worst-case if Communication team also breaks pace by Q3
- **CRO #11:** Diagnose Government 1.67% — is it cycle, capability, or capacity?
- **HoSR #13:** Frame the Q2-Q3 strategic gap (need 78.2M in 8 months = 9.8M/month)
- **Head of Govt Affairs #16:** Specific to your team — root cause + acceleration plan
- **Head of Sales B2B #15:** Martech deal patterns — replicable to bigger team scale?
- **COO #6:** Operational sequencing — which team gets which resources next quarter
- **CWO #5:** Founder-level situational awareness · trigger Op 10 if any agent suggests scope expansion
- **#65 CDA:** Decision velocity check — what decision pack drives Government acceleration?
